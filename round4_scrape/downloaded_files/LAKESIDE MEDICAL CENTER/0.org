        <h2>Community Health Assessments</h2>
        
    


        <p><span style="font-size: 16px;">In 2018, the Health Care District of Palm Beach County Board of Commissioners approved the&nbsp;</span><a class="document pdf" href="http://www.hcdpbc.org/ArticleDocuments/479/Lakeside%20Medical%20Center%202015-2018%20Implementation%20Strategy.pdf.aspx" target="_blank" cm:externalurltype="0" cm:displayas="link" cm:newwindow="true" cm:articlefileid="591" style="background-color: #ffffff; font-size: 16px;">Lakeside Medical Center 2015-2018 Implementation Strategy<span class="file-info">&nbsp;(<span class="file-type">PDF</span>)</span></a><span style="font-size: 16px;">.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>The 2017 <a class="document pdf" href="/ArticleDocuments/12/Community%20Health%20Improvement%20Plan%202017.pdf.aspx" target="_blank">Community Health Improvement Plan (CHIP)<span class="file-info">&nbsp;(<span class="file-type">PDF</span>)</span></a> is a collaborative effort by a diverse group of community leaders, stakeholders, Florida Department of Health in Palm Beach County and the Health Care District of Palm Beach County that serves as a directional roadmap committed to improving the health and quality of life of Palm Beach County residents.</p>
<p>In 2016, the Health Council of Southeast Florida (HCSEF) was enlisted by the Health Care District and the Florida Department of Health in Palm Beach County to facilitate the <a class="document pdf" href="/ArticleDocuments/355/Community%20Health%20Needs%20Assessment%202016.pdf.aspx" target="_blank">Community Health Needs Assessment<span class="file-info">&nbsp;(<span class="file-type">PDF</span>)</span></a> to identify unmet health needs of community residents and to inform and guide future health planning initiatives to meet those needs.</p>
<p>The 2016 <a class="document pdf" href="/ArticleDocuments/471/Glades%20Region%20Health%20Assessment.pdf.aspx" target="_blank" style="background-color: #ffffff;">Glades Region Health Assessment<span class="file-info">&nbsp;(<span class="file-type">PDF</span>)</span></a> is a subset of the overall Palm Beach County Community Health Needs Assessment.</p>
